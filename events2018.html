Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Club Inauguration Ceremony

Date: 01/11/2018

Time: 5:30PM to 7:30PM

Venue: Mini Auditorium, Academic Complex

No. of Students Attended: 120


Title: Inauguration Ceremony

Detailed Report:
The Data Science Artificial Intelligence Club was inaugurated at College of Engineering, Pune on 1st November 2018. Ajinkya Kolhe - Machine Learning Instructor at Google, Prof. Dhamangaonkar - Gymkhana Head, Dr. Yashodhara Haribhakta - Faculty of Department of Computer Engineering and Information Technology and Faculty Advisor of Club were the Chief Guests. Ceremony started with opening note by Prof. Dhamangaonkar. Dr. Haribhakta and Sumedh Pendurkar, Club Secretory presented an introduction of the club. The DSAI Club was Inaugurated by Ajinkya Kolhe, Machine Learning Instructor At Google and also a COEP alumnus. Inauguration was followed by an encouraging talk by Ajinkya Kolhe Sir on "Machine Learning By Self Study" Ceremony was concluded by vote of thanks by Shreyas Dhoot, Administrative Head of the DSAI Club
